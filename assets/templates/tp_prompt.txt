FORMATTING CORRECTIONS TO APPLY:

1. SCENE HEADINGS
   Convert to ALL CAPS. Format: INT./EXT. LOCATION — TIME

2. CHARACTER CUES
   Convert to ALL CAPS on their own line above the dialogue.

4. PARENTHETICALS
   Format on their own line, in parentheses, between the character cue and dialogue.

5. SCENE HEADING DASHES
   Use em dash (—) between location and time: INT. OFFICE — DAY
